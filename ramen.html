Fancy Ramen

A classed-up college staple.

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 Instant Ramen Packet
  • 1 Slice Bacon
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp Green Onion
  • 1 tsp Hot Sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ramen according to the packet.
  2. While the ramen water is boiling, boil enough water to sumerge the egg.
  3. Once the egg water is boiling, boil the egg for 4 minutes. for a soft boiled egg (adjust time for hardness preferance).
  4. While the egg and ramen is boiling, fry the bacon and dice it into small pieces.
  5. Chop the green onion.
  6. Once everything is cooked, add ramen to a bowl, then add the egg, bacon, green onions, and hot sauce.
  7. Enjoy!
