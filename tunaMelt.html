Tuna Melts

A quick and easy lunch to help you power through the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 Slice Bread
  • 1 Pouch Tuna
  • 2 tsp Mayonaise
  • 1 Slices Cheddar Cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Put tuna in a medium size bowl.

    3. Optional Additions: Feel free to mix in whatever you want, like onions, celery, or hot sauce.

  3. Stir in maynoise (more or less to taste).
  4. Evenly spread tuna mixture on the slice of bread.
  5. Place one slice of cheese on the slice of bread.
  6. Place on cookie sheet and cook in oven until the cheese is melty and the bread is browned.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
