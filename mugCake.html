Vanilla Mug Cake

A quick dessert you can whip up in your microwave.

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs Unsalted Butter
  • 3 tbs Milk
  • 0.5 tps Vanilla Extract
  • 3. tbs Sugar
  • 0.25 cup Flour
  • 0.5 tps baking powder
  • 0.25 tps salt

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large mug.
  2. Add the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt).
  3. Throughly mix in wet ingredients (milk and vanilla).
  4. Optional: Add sprinkles or chocolate chips for a fun filling (no need to mix; fillings will disperse in baking).
  5. Mircrowave until top is firm (70-90 seconds).
  6. Optional: Add icecream or frosting
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
