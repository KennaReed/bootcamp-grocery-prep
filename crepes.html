Crepes

Whether your style is sweet or savory, crepes always make a fun and delicious breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 3/2 cups Flour
  • 1 tbs Sugar
  • 1/2 tps Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tps Salt
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 2 tbs Butter
  • 2 tps Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs

Filling Suggestions

Savory

  • Scrambled Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Pesto
  • Avocado

Sweet

  • Powdered Sugar
  • Fresh or Frozen Fuit
  • Syrup
  • Chocolate Sauce or Nutella

Don't forget to mix and match!

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk togeher dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt).
  2. Mix in wet ingreditents in the following order: milk, butter, eggs, then vanilla.
  3. Butter a hot fryinging pan and add 1/4 cup of batter.
  4. Spread batter so evenly distributed across pan.
  5. Cook until underside is brown, then flip. Cook until this side is brown also.
  6. Repeat steps 4-6 until all of the batter is used.

    7. Note: You do not need to add butter for each crepe, about every other is good.

  7. Fill crepes with desired fillings and roll up like a burrito.
  8. Enjoy!
